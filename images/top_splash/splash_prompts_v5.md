## splash_01.png ── January / 1月（雪の畑・冬の静けさ）
**Characters: grandfather + Shiba Inu / 登場: おじいさん + 柴犬**

```
Japanese cozy flat illustration style (ほのぼのイラスト風),
uniform dark warm brown outlines — NOT black, use dark brown (#3d2b1f),
completely flat color fills, zero gradients, zero cel shading,
character skin is near-white with no shading, only soft pink circle blush on cheeks,

PROPORTIONS — CRITICAL:
adult characters must have natural head-to-body proportions (3.5 to 4 head ratio),
do NOT use 2-head chibi or deformed proportions,
body and limbs should feel like real people, not toy figurines,

FACES — CRITICAL:
faces must have clearly drawn eyebrows, a defined nose bridge, and a natural mouth shape,
do NOT reduce facial features to simple dots and minimal curves,
elderly characters must show visible age — wrinkles around eyes and mouth, natural laugh lines,

COMPOSITION — IMPORTANT:
the main character should be shown in three-quarter view or side profile (横顔・斜め顔),
avoid straight-on front-facing portraits — a slight or full turn adds depth and feels more natural,

eyes are simple thin happy arcs (squinting smile expression) with natural eyelid thickness,
plaid/check clothing rendered as simple overlapping color lines,
the Shiba Inu should have realistic dog proportions — not a miniaturized cartoon dog,
square format 480x480px, large rounded corners (radius ~40px),
no text, no letters, no signs anywhere in the image,
style reference: Japanese picture book illustration for adults (大人向け絵本風),
NOT children's sticker art, NOT LINE sticker, NOT yuru-kyara, NOT chibi,

SEASONAL MODE — WINTER:
color palette: muted earthy tones — burnt sienna, camel, khaki, olive, sand beige,
background lighter and more desaturated than foreground — atmospheric depth through tone only,
background elements (trees, farmhouse) have thinner lighter outlines,
sky: pale grayish-blue or soft white, low contrast,

SCENE:
A peaceful winter morning in a rural Japanese vegetable garden.
Elderly grandfather (age 70, thick padded vest, traditional clothing)
shown in three-quarter side view, stands at the garden edge holding a steaming cup of tea,
gazing contentedly at the quiet snowy landscape.
A small Shiba Inu sits beside him in the snow, curly tail, looking up at him warmly.
Background: pale winter sky, bare persimmon tree with a few dried persimmons clinging,
snow lightly covering neat garden beds with frost-hardy leeks and spinach.
Mood: serene, still, the quiet dignity of a winter morning in the countryside.

// 日本の農村、里山、ほのぼのイラスト、絵本風、輪郭線あり、フラット塗り
```
